What is InventorBot?

InventorBot is a functional Discord bot that works seamlessly on your server of choice!

What is Discord?

Discord is an awesome free voice and text server, kinda like Slack and TeamSpeak merged together in awesomeness, and it's free! Did I mention it's free? Anyway, read more here.

How did you make this?!

I made InventorBot in an API called discord.js with Node.JS and JavaScript. They're all pretty cool.

How do I get it to do...stuff?

Once again, easy! Head on over to Add InventorBot and click on the big blue button! It couldn't be easier!

Can I self-host the bot?

Hmm, that's a tough one. The project is open source (psst, check it out), so in theory, yes. However, there is not yet a way to easily do this. Don't worry, @inventor02 is working hard on it :D
